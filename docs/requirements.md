# Requirements

## Project Summary
A Korean clinical evidence search and research support platform for medical trainees and residents.

## Problem
- Clinical questions are hard to answer quickly in busy medical settings.
- Evidence is scattered across papers, case reports, and guidelines.
- Residents often rely on senior advice, which may not always reflect the latest evidence.
- Research data analysis is difficult for users without strong statistics or programming skills.

## Target Users
- Residents
- Medical students
- Fellows
- Early-career specialists

## MVP Scope
- Clinical question search
- Korean papers, case reports, and guidelines prioritized
- AI summary of search results
- Filters for domestic and international evidence
- Rare disease expansion filter

## Out of Scope
- Community features
- User comments/discussion
- Diagnosis confirmation
- Treatment orders
